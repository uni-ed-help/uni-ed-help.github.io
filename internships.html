Internships and Mentorships





Saltz Internship Program
100 hours of visitor interaction and workshops over a summer session

An opportunity for you to meet Museum personnel, explore scientific content, and learn working and teaching skills.

The Science Career Ladders
Museum navigator internship, similar to volunteering dedication.[PAID]

The Science Career Ladder is an education and employment program open to high school students. Students are trained to interact with hands-on exhibits, perform science demonstrations, and help facilitate educational programs and workshops.
Mouse Summer Intern
Computer Science internship for teens with no prior experience. [PAID]

Interns will assist the Mouse staff in summer program coordination with the NYC Department of Education’s STEM Summer in the City Program, for which Mouse provides curriculum, training, and support.

Intern at DNALC
Biotechnology internship within a professional laboratory [PAID]

The DNA Learning Center of Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory has several available positions for highly motivated high school students with an interest in molecular biology and bioinformatics.

Built By Girls: Wave Program
Museum education and employment internship. [PAID]

Students are trained to interact with hands-on exhibits, perform science demonstrations, and help facilitate educational programs and workshops.
Build Mentorship Program
Four year entrepreneurship program [FREE]

BUILD’s mission is to use entrepreneurship to ignite the potential of youth from under-resourced communities and propel them to high school, college & career success.
